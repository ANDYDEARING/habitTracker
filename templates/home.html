Welcome to Habit Tracker

Our Habit Tracker is a simple tool that allows you to set goals for yourself and provides a way to measure whether or not you met your goal on a daily basis. And the best part - it's all done digitally!

Habit tracking is important for three reasons:

  1. Creating a visual cue reminds you to act!
  2. Seeing your progress is a great motivator! You're less likely to miss a day if it will bring your ongoing streak to an end.
  3. Recording your success in the moment can provide a feeling a satisfation that is in itself a reward.
